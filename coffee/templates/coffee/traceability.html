Traceability

Home Traceability

All coffees from Grupo Terruño Nayarita have been 100% traceable since 2006.


ALL of our coffees contain barcoded bag tags, sewn in at origin, furnished with QR Codes that are linked to our TrackYourCoffee website where you will find all microlot information, including but not limited to:
  • Country of Origin
  • Community of Origin
  • Producing Organization
  • Dry Mill
  • Wet Mill(s)
  • Type/Name of Coffee
  • Any Certificates (including organic)
  • Grading Aspect
  • Cup Characteristics
  • Blend Information (if applicable)
    •

The data reported to TrackYourCoffee.com is the tip-of-the-iceberg when compared to the amount of data collected. The information at TrackYourCoffee.com is generated automatically from our Fincalab cloud-based software, based on data collected during the harvest at multiple points and self audited for accuracy.

It begins at reception, each farmer reports what parcella he/she was harvesting each day when delivering ripe fruit to the wet mills/patios. Traceability starts here with ALL farmers that we work with, not just the producing organization. Without data collection and analysis starting at reception in the supply chain, it would be false to claim 100% traceability.


Visit TrackYourCoffee.com to learn more about your microlot from Grupo Terruño Nayarita!
